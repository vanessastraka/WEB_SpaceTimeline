General Information

A solar flare is a suddem, intense burst of radiation from the sun´s surface, usually near sunspots. It occurs when magnetic energy built up in the solar atmosphere is suddenly released. Solar flares can affect radio signals, GPS, and satellites, and are often associated with other solar events like coronal mass ejections.
